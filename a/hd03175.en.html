It is important that Swedish citizenship is given great significance. The value of Swedish citizenship must be respected and upheld to a greater extent than it is today. Therefore, the government is proposing changes that aim to tighten the rules regarding the acquisition of Swedish citizenship, and these changes essentially involve the following:

  • The requirements for residency in Sweden are being tightened.
  • Higher requirements are being placed on the lifestyle of those who wish to acquire Swedish citizenship.
  • A requirement for financial self-sufficiency is being introduced when applying for citizenship.
  • A requirement for sufficient knowledge of Swedish and of Swedish society is being introduced in order to obtain Swedish citizenship.
  • The application process is being streamlined as much as possible.
  • It is being made possible for children to independently apply for and acquire Swedish citizenship.

In order to ensure that Swedish law is in compliance with EU law, it is also proposed that former Swedish citizens who were born outside of Sweden and have never resided in the country should be able to apply to regain their Swedish citizenship.

The proposed changes to the law are scheduled to come into effect on June 6, 2026. However, the changes regarding the citizenship test in reading and listening comprehension in Swedish at a functional level are proposed to come into effect on October 1, 2027, or on an earlier date determined by the government. The changes regarding the citizenship test in Swedish in other respects are proposed to come into effect on a date determined by the government.
