Full text

In this document, the government presents its assessment of the observations and recommendations made by the Swedish National Audit Office (Riksrevisionen) in the report "Industriklivet - Planning, Implementation, and Follow-up" (RiR 2024:17). The Swedish National Audit Office's overall conclusion is that both the government's design of, and the Swedish Energy Agency's work on, Industriklivet have several shortcomings. The Swedish National Audit Office therefore assesses that there is a risk that Industriklivet will not effectively contribute to either the industrial sector's transition to a low-carbon economy or Sweden's climate goals.

The government welcomes the Swedish National Audit Office's review of Industriklivet and agrees with some of the recommendations made. On January 16, 2025, the government tasked the Swedish Environmental Protection Agency, in collaboration with the Swedish Energy Agency, to review any potential overlap between Klimatklivet and Industriklivet, and to propose adjustments to the respective support regulations if necessary.

With this document, the government considers the Swedish National Audit Office's report to be finalized.
