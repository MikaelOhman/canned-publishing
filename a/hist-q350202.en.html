Erik Eriksson, including variations such as Erik/Eric and Eriksson/Ericsson/Erikson/Ericson, may refer to:

  • Erik Eriksson (king) (1216–1250): Swedish king, also known as "Erik the Scholar" and "Erik the Lame."
  • Erik Eriksson (Gyllenstierna), senior (born in the 1470s): Swedish lawman and military officer.
  • Erik Eriksson (Gyllenstierna), junior (died 1502): Swedish knight and commander of Älfsborg fortress.
  • Erik Eriksson (surveyor) in 17th-century Västerbotten.
  • Erick Ericksson (1695–1761): Radical Pietist, see Skevikarna.
  • Erik Eriksson (1718–1769): Mayor and member of parliament.
  • Eric Ericsson (1763–1828): Member of parliament and industrialist.
  • Eric Ericsson in Ruda (1787–1863): Swedish large-scale farmer from Östergötland.
  • Eric Erickson (spy) (1890–1983): Swedish businessman and spy.
  • Eric Erickson (1892–1965): Swedish-American baseball player.
  • Erik H. Erikson (1902–1994): Jewish-American psychoanalyst.
  • Erik Eriksson (competitive cyclist) (1905–1999): Competitive cyclist.
  • Erik "Saxjerker" Eriksson (1906–1962): Swedish jazz musician.
  • Erik Eriksson (hydrologist) (1917–2019): Swedish hydrologist.
  • Eric Ericson (conductor) (1918–2013): Swedish choir conductor.
  • Erik Eriksson (filmmaker) (1933–2005): Swedish documentary filmmaker.
  • Erik Eriksson (author) (1937–2021): Swedish journalist and author.
  • Eric Ericson (author) (born 1972): Swedish author and designer.
  • Eric Ericson (actor) (born 1974): Swedish actor.
  • Erick Erickson (born 1975): American conservative blogger and TV commentator.
  • Erik Eriksson (cross-country skier): Swedish cross-country skier, grandson of Nils "Mora-Nisse" Karlsson.
  • Erik Eriksson (triple jumper) (born 1980): Swedish triple jumper.
  • Erik Eriksson (discus thrower): Swedish discus thrower, from the 1890s.
