TL;DRThe group working to combat crime in Flen held a meeting to discuss how to improve safety in the municipality. They talked about a wide range of issues, from the location of the police station to plans for preventing drug and alcohol abuse.
In plain language

The crime prevention committee in Flen held a meeting on May 25, 2026. The group consists of politicians who work together with municipal officials to reduce crime.

During the meeting, they received information about the current situation in the municipality and within the police force. Iris Pettersson, the head of social services, and Anders Iwerbo, the chairman of the municipality's property company, presented their work. They discussed, among other things, how to improve knowledge among those working on these issues.

They also discussed a plan to address drugs, alcohol, narcotics, and other harmful substances, often referred to as an ANDTS action plan. The group also reviewed how to create better plans to combat crime and how to communicate this information to other parts of the municipality.

The next major piece of work, scheduled for completion by August 31, aims to ensure that all key stakeholders in the municipality are aware of the progress being made and what needs to be done moving forward.
