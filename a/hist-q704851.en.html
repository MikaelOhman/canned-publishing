Wolfgang Meusslin (also known as "Musculus" Meusslin), born September 10, 1497, and died August 30, 1563, was a professor of theology in Bern. He is represented in the Swedish hymnal of 1986 with one work (number 185), which was originally written in Latin in the 3rd century by Ambrose of Milan: "Christe, qui lux es et dies."
