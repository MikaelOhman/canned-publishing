The Swedish Civil Defence Agency – a new name for the Agency for Societal Protection and Preparedness.

Source Riksdagen Read the original at Riksdagen →2025-09-08

The proposal suggests that the Swedish Civil Contingency Agency (MSB) should change its name to the Agency for Civil Defence. The new name aims to clarify and emphasize the agency's mission within civil defence. In light of the name change, the government proposes amendments to the laws that currently contain the agency's name.

The proposed amendments are scheduled to come into effect on January 1, 2026.

Proposed Resolutions for the Parliament

Prop. 2024/25:205

  1. The Parliament approves the government's proposal for an amendment to the Environmental Code.

  4. The Parliament approves the government's proposal for an amendment to the Public Access and Secrecy Act (2009:400).

  6. The Parliament approves the government's proposal for an amendment to the Act (2010:1011) on flammable and explosive goods.
  7. The Parliament approves the government's proposal for an amendment to the Customs Act (2016:253).
  8. The Parliament approves the government's proposal for an amendment to the Act (2016:534) on measures for the expansion of broadband networks.
  9. The Parliament approves the government's proposal for an amendment to the Act (2021:319) on the Swedish Transport Agency's accident database.
