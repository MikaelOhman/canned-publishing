Klas Horn is the name of several historical figures:

  • Klas Henriksson Horn (Died in the 1520s), a lawman and member of the Swedish Council of State.

  • Clas Fredrik Horn af Åminne (1763-1823).
  • Claes Fredrik Horn af Åminne (1791–1865).

And also three ships:
