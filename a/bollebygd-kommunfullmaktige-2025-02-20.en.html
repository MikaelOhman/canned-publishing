Municipal Council Meeting, Bollebygds Municipality, February 20, 2025.

Source Bollebygds kommun Read the original at Bollebygds kommun ã2025-02-20
TL;DRThe municipal council of Bollebygd has received a proposal to equip the vehicles used by home care services with mobile phone holders.
In plain language

A resident has submitted a citizen proposal regarding the working conditions of home care staff. The proposal suggests that vehicles used by home care services should be equipped with technology to allow mobile phones to be connected and used hands-free while driving.

The municipal council, which is the highest decision-making body in the municipality, has received this proposal. They have decided to forward the matter to the social welfare committee. The social welfare committee is the part of the municipality responsible for issues related to care and home services.

Now, the social welfare committee will examine the issue further and decide whether to purchase or install these holders in the vehicles.
