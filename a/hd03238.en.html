New agency for environmental impact assessments.

In plain language

The government has appointed a person to prepare for the creation of a new agency. This agency will be responsible for environmental assessments, meaning it will ensure that various projects and activities comply with environmental laws. The plan is for the new agency to begin operations on July 1, 2027.

Currently, the environmental assessment work is carried out by the environmental assessment divisions of the county administrative boards. This means that the local authorities in each county are responsible for assessing environmental issues. The new agency will take over all of this work from the county administrative boards.

In order for this to happen, the Parliament must amend several laws, including the Environmental Code. The Environmental Code is the law that contains the most important rules for how we should protect nature and the environment in Sweden. The government has sent a proposal to the Parliament to obtain approval for these changes.

Some of the new rules are proposed to come into effect as early as September 1, 2026. The remaining changes to the laws will come into effect at the same time as the new agency starts operating, that is, on July 1, 2027.

Full text

The government has appointed a special investigator to prepare and implement the establishment of a new environmental assessment authority, which is scheduled to begin operations on July 1, 2027. In the proposal, the government suggests the necessary legislative changes to enable the authority to take over the tasks currently performed by the environmental assessment divisions of the county administrative boards.

The government also proposes a change to a provision regarding the extension of validity. This change is proposed to take effect on September 1, 2026. The remaining legislative changes are proposed to take effect on July 1, 2027.
