Supplementary budget for 2025 – Additional support for Ukraine.

Source Riksdagen Read the original at Riksdagen →2025-04-01

In this proposal, the government is presenting proposed changes to the state budget for 2025. These proposals are a consequence of the deteriorating security situation in Sweden's vicinity, due to Russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine.

The proposal includes suggestions for increased authorizations to procure and subsequently donate equipment and supplies that have been requested by Ukraine. It also contains proposals for authorizations allowing the government to donate defense materiel that the Swedish Armed Forces can temporarily spare. Furthermore, the proposal includes suggestions for increased funding to prepare the materiel that is to be donated, as well as increased funding and authorizations to replace parts of the materiel that is proposed to be donated to Ukraine. Additionally, the proposal includes suggestions for support in the maritime sector, emergency services, and energy. Moreover, the proposal includes suggestions to strengthen Ukraine's total defense capabilities and protect democratic processes in Ukraine. The proposal also includes suggestions to reallocate funds that, according to the proposed budget for 2025, have been allocated and calculated for support to Ukraine (the so-called "Ukraine framework") from 2026 to 2025.
