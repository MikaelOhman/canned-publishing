The government presents in this report an overview of the progress towards the youth policy goal that all young people should have good living conditions, the ability to shape their lives, and influence on societal development. The development of young people's living conditions is presented based on the aspects of health, education, work, economy, housing, crime and vulnerability to crime, and leisure and organization.

The report also outlines prioritized areas within youth policy: to increase the mental and physical health of young people, and their social inclusion and integration into the labor market, and to ensure that all young people have a meaningful and safe leisure time and are involved in building society.

The report also includes an account of international cooperation in youth policy.
