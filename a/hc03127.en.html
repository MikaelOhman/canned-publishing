New regulations are being introduced regarding the amount that can be purchased. Individuals are only allowed to buy a maximum of 18 grams of nitrous oxide at a time, and a maximum of two containers. It is also prohibited to sell nitrous oxide to individuals under the age of 18.

Those who sell nitrous oxide must report this to the Public Health Agency, which is the authority responsible for ensuring that the regulations are followed. If someone sells nitrous oxide in violation of the rules, they may face fines or imprisonment for up to six months.

Full text

The proposal suggests a new law regarding nitrous oxide (laughing gas). The purpose of the law is to prevent the use of nitrous oxide as a recreational drug. Nitrous oxide should not be sold or otherwise distributed in commercial settings if there is a reasonable suspicion that it will be used for intoxication purposes. A maximum of 18 grams of nitrous oxide may be sold or otherwise distributed to a private individual per transaction. This sale or distribution should be limited to a maximum of two containers, each containing no more than 9 grams of nitrous oxide. Nitrous oxide should not be sold or otherwise distributed in commercial settings to individuals under the age of 18. The import of nitrous oxide is also regulated. The marketing of nitrous oxide is prohibited if it violates the prohibitions and restrictions outlined in the law.

Any business wishing to retail nitrous oxide must register its sales with the Public Health Agency. The Public Health Agency will oversee the majority of the provisions in the new law and the regulations issued in connection with it.

Anyone who intentionally or negligently sells nitrous oxide in violation of the law's provisions will be prosecuted for illegal handling of nitrous oxide, and may be subject to fines or imprisonment for up to six months.

The new regulations are proposed to come into effect on July 1, 2025. The retail sale of nitrous oxide will be permitted until September 1, 2025, without requiring prior registration.
