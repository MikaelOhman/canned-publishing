The Swedish National Audit Office's overall conclusion is that the dental care subsidy system is not effective. The Swedish National Audit Office makes four recommendations to the government, as well as three to the Dental and Pharmaceutical Benefits Agency and one to the Consumer Agency.

The government welcomes the Swedish National Audit Office's review and notes that it highlights the need to develop the state-funded dental care system to make it more effective. Given the extensive measures that the government has already taken, which largely align with the review's analysis and proposals, and with the intention of taking further action in the areas identified by the Swedish National Audit Office, the government agrees only partially with the Swedish National Audit Office's observations and recommendations.
