Full text

The proposal includes suggestions aimed at enabling more people to charge their electric vehicles near their homes.

The proposals stipulate that a tenant or homeowner should have the right to request the installation of a charging point for charging an electric vehicle in the parking space associated with their residence, provided the parking space is located in the same building as the apartment or in close proximity to it. The resident will be responsible for the costs associated with the charging point. It should only be possible to refuse the installation if there is a valid reason. The proposals are based on an EU directive.

The proposed amendments to the law are scheduled to come into effect on May 29, 2026.
