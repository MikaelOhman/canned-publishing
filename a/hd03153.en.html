Full text

In this document, the government presents its assessment of the observations and recommendations made by the Swedish National Audit Office (Riksrevisionen) in the report "International Climate Efforts – Unclear Goals and Uncertain Outcomes" (RiR 2025:28).

The Swedish National Audit Office's overall assessment is that international climate efforts are partially achieving their intended goals. The audit office assesses that these efforts have the potential to contribute to Sweden's climate targets in a cost-effective manner, as well as to the transition to a climate-neutral economy and sustainable development. The Swedish National Audit Office also emphasizes that the results of international climate efforts are uneven, the monitoring is inadequate, and that the government's goal-setting is weak.

The government welcomes the Swedish National Audit Office's report as a valuable basis for the continued work of implementing cost-effective international climate efforts. The government agrees with some of the Swedish National Audit Office's recommendations. The government will task the Swedish Energy Agency to take measures to ensure the cost-effectiveness of international climate efforts. The government is also reviewing the reporting requirements for the Swedish Energy Agency's work on international climate efforts and will, in dialogue with the agency, explore opportunities for improvement to strengthen monitoring.

With this document, the government considers the Swedish National Audit Office's report to be finalized.
