Full text

In this document, the government presents its assessment and the measures taken based on the observations made by the Swedish National Audit Office (Riksrevisionen) in the report "Supervision of Medical Devices – Priorities, Implementation, and Follow-up" (RiR 2024:23).

The Swedish National Audit Office has reviewed the effectiveness of the state's supervision of medical devices. The audit office provides three recommendations to the government and a number of recommendations to the Swedish Medical Products Agency (Läkemedelsverket) and the Swedish Inspectorate for Healthcare and Welfare (IVO).

The government welcomes the Swedish National Audit Office's review and acknowledges that it makes an important contribution to the work of achieving effective supervision of medical devices. In response to the recommendations made by the Swedish National Audit Office, the government has tasked the Swedish Medical Products Agency and IVO with examining the possibility of transferring responsibility for the supervision of self-manufactured medical devices from IVO to the Swedish Medical Products Agency. Furthermore, the government has tasked the Swedish Medical Products Agency with reviewing the model for annual fees for the agency's medical device activities and with disseminating information and support regarding the regulations for medical devices to the medical device industry, healthcare, and social services.

With this document, the government considers the Swedish National Audit Office's review report to be finalized.
