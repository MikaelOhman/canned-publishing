TL;DRStarting May 8, 2026, a period will begin during which you can change the funds in your supplementary pension plan. Most changes will be implemented by May 13th.
In plain language

Between May 8th and May 13th, 2026, the Swedish Pensions Agency will open a window for you to manage your funds within your supplementary pension plan. This means that during these days, you can choose to move your money between different investment funds, which are the investments that make up your pension.

The purchase of new funds begins on Monday, May 11th, and will be finalized for most people by Wednesday, May 13th. If you want to make a change, you must submit your request no later than Thursday evening, May 7th.

This is an annual opportunity to adjust how your money is invested. It's important to keep track of the dates if you want to move your money to a different fund than the one you currently have.
