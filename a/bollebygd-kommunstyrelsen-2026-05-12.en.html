TL;DRThe municipal council of Bollebygd has made several decisions covering a wide range of issues, from new safety regulations to planning for future facilities and roads. They have also approved financial support for various political parties.
In plain language

The municipality has developed new regulations for protecting information and managing digital security. There is also a new plan for transportation within the municipality, known as a mobility plan, and a program to create a safe and positive environment for all residents.

Regarding buildings and land, discussions have taken place about a preliminary study for the first phase of a new municipal building. They have also reviewed a plan for how the municipality's premises will be used between 2027 and 2036, as well as a land exchange in an area called Buagärde.

Several political parties, including the Green Party, the Center Party, the People's Voice, and the Moderates, have had their financial party subsidies approved for 2026. Annual reports for various organizations have also been reviewed, such as the foundation that manages rental apartments in Bollebygd and the local fire department.

There have also been discussions about citizen proposals, such as the possibility of a building for activities for retirees or a new bike path along Viaredssjön. In addition, discussions have taken place about new regulations for how those holding political positions in the municipality will be compensated for their work during the new period until 2030.

Ärendet Bollebygds kommuns informationssäkerhets-, cybersäkerhets- och dataskyddspolicy beskriver kommunens övergripande viljeinriktning och principer för arbetet med att skydda information och personuppgifter. Policyn omfattar kommunens informationstillgångar, vilket inkluderar verksamhetens information samt resurser kopplade till informationshantering, såsom IT-system, data, medarbetare och teknisk infrastruktur.

Alla kommunens verksamheter hanterar information om invånare, företag, organisationer och den egna verksamheten. Informationen ska hanteras på ett säkert och korrekt sätt eftersom den utgör en grund för tillit och förtroende för kommunen.
