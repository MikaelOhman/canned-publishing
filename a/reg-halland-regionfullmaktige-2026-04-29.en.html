Regional Assembly Member, Region Halland, April 29, 2026.

Source Region Halland Read the original at Region Halland →2026-04-29
TL;DRThe regional council of Halland has made decisions on important issues related to healthcare, the working environment for employees, and how the region will be governed until 2030.
In plain language

During the meeting, several proposals related to healthcare were discussed. These included offering more women screenings for breast cancer, improving efforts to combat cervical cancer, and implementing regular tests for prostate cancer.

The region also made decisions about its future direction. This includes new regulations regarding how members (individuals who participate in meetings and make decisions) can operate, as well as the organizational structure between the years 2027 and 2030.

Decisions were made concerning employee rights, such as pensions and protection during restructuring. Additionally, the annual report for 2025 was approved, and decisions were made regarding how funds will be allocated next year, as well as how to address threats and violence against employees.
