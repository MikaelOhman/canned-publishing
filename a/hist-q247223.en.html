Full text

Filip or Philip is a male name of Greek origin, derived from Philippos, meaning 'friend of horses'. During the Middle Ages, the form Filpus was used. The female equivalent is Filippa.

In Sweden, the name has been very popular as a given name since the 1980s. Between 1998 and 2001, Filip fluctuated between first and second place on the popularity charts. As of December 31, 2013, there were a total of 40,814 men in Sweden named Filip or Philip, of whom 28,558 used it as their preferred name. In 2011, 614 boys were given the name, with 423 using it as their nickname. Approximately two-thirds (17,906) of those who use Filip as a nickname spell it with an "F".

Name day: In Sweden, May 2nd. In the Swedish-speaking Finnish calendar of names, Filip's name day is March 8th, since the introduction of a separate calendar for Swedish speakers in Finland in 1929.
