TL;DRThe Social Welfare Committee in Orsa proposes new regulations for how decisions are made within the municipality. This is due to the introduction of new laws regarding activity requirements for individuals receiving financial assistance.
In plain language

The Social Welfare Committee has proposed changes to the municipality's regulations regarding who is authorized to make decisions. This is necessary to adapt the work to new laws from the national parliament. Starting July 1, 2026, there is a requirement for all recipients of financial assistance, also known as social assistance, to engage in some form of activity.

To meet these new requirements, the municipal board needs to amend its internal guidelines, known as delegation orders. This means clarifying which decisions can be made and how they should be made according to the new social services law. The proposal is now being forwarded to the municipal board for a final decision.

Within the social services department, work is currently underway to hire temporary staff and personnel for home care services to meet the needs of the summer season. They have also reviewed information about how reports of concern for children and young people are handled. It is evident that there are many cases to manage within the work related to children and young people, but the situation is stable.

In addition to the new regulations, the committee has also reviewed reports on how the work has progressed during the month of May. They have also approved a list of decisions made by staff during the week of June 11, 2026.

§ 82 KS 2026/145

Delegationsordning för kommunstyrelsen - Revideringar 2026 Beslut Socialutskottet föreslår kommunstyrelsen att anta de föreslagna förändringarna i delegationsordningen. Sammanfattning av ärendet Riksdagen har fattat beslut om ett obligatoriskt aktivitetskrav för alla mottagare av ekonomiskt bistånd (försörjningsstöd). Reformen som är en central del av regeringens bidragsreform, syftar till att öka individers möjligheter att få ett arbete och stärka deras språkliga förutsättningar. De nya lagändringarna och kraven började gälla den 1 juli 2026. Med anledning av de nya kraven som ställs gällande aktivitetskravet finns behov av att komplettera Kommunstyrelsens delegationsordning enligt förslag. Det innebär att viss text i befintligt lagrum behöver förtydligas och att nya beslut ska upprättas enligt Socialtjänstlagen 2025:400.
