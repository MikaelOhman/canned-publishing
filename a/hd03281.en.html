Full text

In this document, the government presents its assessment of the observations and the recommendations made by the Swedish National Audit Agency (Riksrevisionen) in the report "Changing Circumstances – Inefficient Handling in Social Security" (RiR 2026:4). The Swedish National Audit Agency has reviewed the Swedish Social Insurance Agency's (Försäkringskassan) handling of changes in circumstances related to disability benefits and housing allowances, and the Swedish Pensions Agency's (Pensionsmyndigheten) corresponding handling of housing allowances for pensioners.

The Swedish National Audit Agency's overall conclusion is that the Swedish Social Insurance Agency and the Swedish Pensions Agency have several efficiency shortcomings in the handling of changing circumstances, and that the current procedures pose risks of incorrect payments. The Swedish National Audit Agency makes a recommendation to the government and a number of recommendations to the Swedish Social Insurance Agency and the Swedish Pensions Agency.

The government welcomes the Swedish National Audit Agency's review and believes that it makes an important contribution to highlighting the need for improvements in this area, so that the processing of social security benefits can be made more efficient and the number of incorrect payments can be reduced. However, given the measures the government has taken to strengthen the authorities' ability to prevent, avoid, and detect incorrect payments, and to collect and exchange information, the government does not currently consider it necessary to initiate the legislative work that the Swedish National Audit Agency recommends.

With this document, the government considers the Swedish National Audit Agency's report to be finalized.
