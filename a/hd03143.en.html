Full text

In this proposal, the government presents proposed changes to the state budget for 2026. These proposals are primarily a consequence of the serious geopolitical situation in Sweden's vicinity and the deteriorating economic situation in Ukraine, due to Russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine.

The proposal includes a provision authorizing the government to donate grenade launchers with ammunition to Ukraine, which the Swedish Armed Forces can temporarily dispense with. The proposal also includes suggestions for increased funding and authorizations to acquire, develop, and prepare equipment for donation to Ukraine, as well as to replace a portion of the equipment proposed to be donated to the country. Furthermore, the proposal includes suggestions for increased funding to support Ukraine in strengthening the country's total defense capabilities. The proposal also suggests that Sweden provide a guarantee for a loan from the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development to Ukraine.

The proposal also includes suggestions to ensure Sweden's preparedness for vaccinations during influenza pandemics.
