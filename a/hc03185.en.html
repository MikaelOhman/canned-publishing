In plain language

The government proposes a change to the state budget for 2025. This involves a capital injection – meaning the state will provide funds – to the wholly state-owned company Apotek Produktion & Laboratorier AB (APL).

The funds are needed for APL to acquire a business that manufactures certain particularly critical medications. This type of manufacturing is important to ensure that Sweden can maintain its supply of medicines even during crises, periods of heightened alert, and, in the worst-case scenario, war.

The government wants the parliament's approval to decide on a contribution of up to 700 million Swedish krona.

Full text

In this proposal, the government is presenting proposed changes to the state budget for 2025. The state-owned company Apotek Produktion & Laboratorier AB requires additional capital to acquire a business that manufactures certain critical pharmaceuticals. These pharmaceuticals are essential for Sweden's manufacturing capabilities during peacetime crises, heightened alert levels, and, ultimately, wartime.

The proposal includes a suggestion that the government be authorized to decide on capital injections for Apotek Produktion & Laboratorier AB, up to a maximum of 700,000,000 Swedish krona.
