Defense Industry Strategy for a Stronger Sweden: Innovation, Production, and Collaboration.

Source Riksdagen Read the original at Riksdagen →2025-06-12

The government has developed a defense industry strategy. In this strategy, the government outlines its approach to strengthening Sweden's defense industrial base by promoting an innovative and competitive defense industry with high production capacity.

The defense industry strategy is based on the national security strategy and the defense policy guidelines in the government proposal "Total Defense 2025-2030" (prop. 2024/25:34, bet. 2024/25:FöU2, rskr. 2024/25:114). The strategy outlines measures that build upon the approach described by the government in the strategic guidelines for defense innovation (Fö2024/00023) and in "The Role of Space in a New Security Landscape – Sweden's Defense and Security Strategy for Space" (Fö2024/01325).

In the defense industry strategy, the government outlines three priorities: * Innovation * Production * Collaboration

The defense industry strategy presents concrete measures to strengthen the business sector's ability to contribute to Sweden's security. In parallel, the government has developed an industrial strategy that addresses goals and conditions for Swedish industry in a broader sense. The strategies complement each other and, together, contribute to a more resilient and secure Sweden.
