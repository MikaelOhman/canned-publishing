TL;DRThe government wants to simplify the rules for teachers and preschool teachers so that their qualifications can be changed without them having to apply to the Swedish National Agency for Education. The proposal aims to make administration easier for the teaching profession.
In plain language

The government proposes an amendment to the Education Act, which is scheduled to come into effect on July 1, 2026. The purpose is to make it easier for teachers and preschool teachers to have their updated qualifications registered.

Currently, teachers often have to apply to the National Agency for Education to change their qualifications. With the new rule, the government will be able to determine that certain changes can be made automatically without a new application. This will be done if it simplifies the work for teachers and is based on new regulations in the law.

The proposal also means that the government can determine that certain types of qualifications do not need to be included on the actual certificate. This applies to qualifications that are only valid for a limited time, so-called transitional periods.

If the proposal is approved, the new rules will be in effect from the summer of 2026. The goal is to reduce the administrative burden for those working in schools and preschools.

Full text

The proposal suggests introducing an information clause in the Education Act (2010:800) stating that the government, with the support of its remaining authority, can issue regulations regarding exemptions from certain provisions of the law concerning the qualifications and certifications of teachers and preschool teachers. This new information clause means that the government can issue regulations stating that a teacher's or preschool teacher's eligibility to teach in the school system can be modified or expanded without an application to the National Agency for Education, provided that such changes are a necessary consequence of changes in the Education Act and in regulations regarding the requirements for being eligible to teach in the school system. A prerequisite for the government to issue such regulations is that such a procedure facilitates matters for teachers or preschool teachers. The new information clause also means that the government can issue regulations stating that a teacher's or preschool teacher's eligibility to teach in the school system, which only applies during a transitional period, does not need to be indicated on the certification.

The proposed amendment is to come into force on July 1, 2026.

Government Proposal: The Riksdag (Swedish Parliament) adopts the government's proposal for an amendment to the Education Act (2010:800). 3

Prop. 2025/26:149 2 Proposed Amendment to the Education Act (2010:800)

Hereby, it is stipulated that section 16 of chapter 2 of the Education Act (2010:800) shall have the following wording.

Current Wording | Proposed Wording ---|---

  1. |

| 16 § 1 | 16 § 1 The Swedish National Agency for Education shall, upon application, grant a teaching license to a teacher or preschool teacher who holds a qualification that confers eligibility. | The Swedish National Agency for Education shall, upon application, grant a teaching license to a teacher or preschool teacher who holds a qualification that confers eligibility. A teaching license shall not be granted if the circumstances are such that the license would have been revoked according to section 23 had the applicant been licensed. | The Swedish National Agency for Education shall, upon application, supplement a teacher's or preschool teacher's teaching license with qualifications beyond those conferred by a qualification mentioned in the first paragraph, if the teacher or preschool teacher has achieved satisfactory results in further qualification-granting education. This applies regardless of when the education was completed. In the same way, the Swedish National Agency for Education shall supplement the license with further qualifications if the teacher or preschool teacher is deemed to have such qualifications on other grounds. The teacher's or preschool teacher's qualifications to provide instruction in the school system shall be stated on the teaching license. | The government or the authority that the government designates may, with reference to section 7 of chapter 8 of the Instrument of Government, issue regulations:

  1. regarding the fact that a teacher's or preschool teacher's qualifications to provide instruction in the school system may be changed or expanded without an application to the Swedish National Agency for Education, when such changes are a necessary consequence of changes in this Act and in regulations referred to in section 2, paragraph 2, and such a procedure facilitates matters for teachers or preschool teachers, and
  2. regarding the fact that a teacher's or preschool teacher's qualifications to provide instruction in the school system, which only apply for a transitional period, do not need to be stated on the teaching license.

This Act shall enter into force on July 1, 2026.
