Increased property tax for wind turbines.

Source Riksdagen Read the original at Riksdagen →2025-06-17

The proposal suggests increasing the property tax on wind turbines from 0.2% to 0.5% of the assessed value. Property tax is payable on properties that, during the property assessment process, are classified as electricity generation units. For electricity generation units consisting of a property unit with wind turbines, the property tax is 0.2% of the assessed value. For other electricity generation units, the property tax is 0.5% of the assessed value. The proposal means that the tax rate will be uniform for all electricity generation units.

The proposed amendment to the law is scheduled to come into effect on January 1, 2026.
