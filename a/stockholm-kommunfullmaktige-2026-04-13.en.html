In plain language

The City of Stockholm has decided to invest in improving Lillholmskolan school in Skärholmen. This is a significant investment of 461.7 million Swedish krona, which will be used to renovate and expand the school building.

A key part of the project is the creation of a new sports hall at the school. This is being done in collaboration with various departments within the city to provide better facilities for both students and athletes.

This decision also includes adapting how the school's operations will function after the construction is complete. The entire project is being managed by the company Skolfastigheter i Stockholm AB.
