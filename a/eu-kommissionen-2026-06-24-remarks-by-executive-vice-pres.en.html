The European Commission has proposed two new regulations, designed to strengthen Europol and Eurojust, two key European agencies.

Europol is the agency responsible for combating crime across Europe through cooperation. Eurojust is an agency that assists national courts and prosecutors in cooperating on major criminal cases.

By strengthening these two agencies, the aim is to make it easier to stop criminals who operate across borders. The decision was presented by Vice-President Virkkunen in Brussels on June 24, 2026.
