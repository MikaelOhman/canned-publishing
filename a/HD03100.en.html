Full text

The proposal contains the government's proposed guidelines for economic and budgetary policy. The purpose of these guidelines is to outline the direction of policy in preparation for the budget proposal for 2027.

The guidelines for economic policy are based on the other content of the proposal, which includes a description of the overall direction of fiscal policy, an assessment of macroeconomic developments and the finances of the public sector, an account of the challenges facing employment policy, as well as the importance of productivity and public investment, a follow-up and assessment of the budgetary policy objectives, a follow-up of the state budget, and an assessment of the long-term sustainability of fiscal policy.

The proposal also includes suggestions for certain changes to the classification of expenditure areas.
