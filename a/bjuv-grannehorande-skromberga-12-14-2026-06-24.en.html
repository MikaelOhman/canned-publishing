TL;DRNeighbors of the property located at Skromberga 12:14 in Bjuv have been given the opportunity to express their opinions on a proposal to change the intended use of a building. This is because the area does not yet have a finalized zoning plan that precisely defines what activities are permitted there.
In plain language

The Building and Environmental Department of Bjuvs Municipality has received a building permit application for the property Skromberga 12:14. The application concerns a change of use, meaning the applicant wants to use a building in a way that is different from its current purpose.

Since the area is located outside of what is known as a detailed planning area, the municipality needs to consult with the neighbors to gather their opinions. A detailed plan is a document that specifies what can be built and done in different locations within a municipality. When such a plan is absent, it is particularly important that those living nearby have the opportunity to express their views.

If you live in the vicinity and would like to review the documents related to this matter, you can contact the Building and Environmental Department. You can send an email to bygg@bjuv.se or call 042-458 50 00. Please remember to mention the case number BYGG-2026-87 so they know which matter you are referring to.

If you wish to submit your opinion or complaint, you must send it to the municipality by July 8, 2026. You can send your response via email or by regular mail to Bjuvs Municipality, Building and Environmental Department, Mejerigatan 3.
