Full text

The government's proposal includes changes to the law on total defense duty and the law on protection of employment for certain service within the total defense system, among other things. The proposals include the following:

  • The obligation to complete military service is extended for those who have been called up for duty during a period of heightened readiness, as well as for previously employed professional officers and reserve officers, regardless of the level of readiness.
  • The period of service in the Swedish Armed Forces' reserve remains in effect until the end of the calendar year in which the person subject to total defense duty turns 47 years old.
  • The total time for readiness service is changed to a maximum of 360 days, distributed over one or more periods of service. One period of service may last up to 230 days.
  • The individual's right to leave from their civilian employment during service in the Swedish Armed Forces is clarified in the law.

The proposed changes to the law are scheduled to come into effect on January 1, 2027.

  2. The Parliament adopts the government's proposal for an amendment to the law (1994:2076) on protection of employment for certain service within the total defense system, etc.
