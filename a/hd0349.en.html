Organized crime poses a threat to the entire system and must be prevented and combated with the full capacity of society. To be able to intervene at an early stage and prevent serious crimes from being completed, the government proposes to expand criminal liability for attempted crimes, preparation, and incitement.

The expanded liability for attempted crimes is proposed to take effect on April 1, 2026. The other proposed amendments to the law are proposed to take effect on July 1, 2026.
