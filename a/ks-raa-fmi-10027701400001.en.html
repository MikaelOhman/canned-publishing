Full text

Runestone, 1.7 m high, 0.8-1.0 m wide, and 0.3 m thick. The height of the runes is 6-12 cm. The runestone faces west with its inscribed surface. Part of the northern rune loop is damaged. It has been photographed. U785. Adjacent to and below runestone 1: A grave marker, made of limestone, 0.4 m high. At the top, there is a depression, 9 cm in diameter and 6 cm thick. An inscription resembling a chain runs around the grave marker, with a width of 3-4 cm. This description has not been quality checked. Information may be missing, inaccurate, or outdated. Please refer to the inventory book for more information.

Location: Sweden, Uppsala, Enköping, Uppland, Tillinge.
