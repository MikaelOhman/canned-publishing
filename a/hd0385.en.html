Expediency and deadlines for preliminary review in the re-examination of procurement processes for funds used in the premium pension fund market.

Source Riksdagen Read the original at Riksdagen →2025-12-16

The proposal suggests introducing regulations regarding requirements for speed and limitation periods in cases involving judicial review of procurement processes and the validity of fund agreements, according to the Act (2022:760) on the procurement of funds for the premium pension fund market.

The main purpose of the proposals is to improve the efficiency of the courts' handling of such cases.

The proposed amendments to the law are intended to come into force on July 1, 2026.
