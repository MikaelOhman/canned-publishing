Stricter requirements for public defenders and increased competence requirements for interpreters in immigration cases.

Source Riksdagen Read the original at Riksdagen →2025-06-25

It is crucial for legal certainty that the work of public defenders maintains a high quality. It must be ensured that public defenders possess the necessary competence for their assignments, particularly in immigration proceedings, but also in other cases and matters. It is also important that the interpreters and translators employed in immigration proceedings provide high-quality interpretation and translation services. Therefore, the government's proposal includes legislative amendments regarding the following:

* The eligibility requirements for public defenders will be tightened. * A special permit will be required for a public defender to delegate their responsibilities to another person (substitution). * Unsuitable representatives and assistants will be able to be declared ineligible to participate in immigration cases at the Swedish Migration Agency, the Police Authority, and the Security Police. * Competency requirements will be introduced for interpreters and translators in immigration cases.

The proposed amendments to the law will come into effect on January 1, 2026.
