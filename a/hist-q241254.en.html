Full text

Several Swedish royals are known by the name Sofia of Sweden:

  • Sofia Eriksdotter, princess, daughter of King Erik Knutsson, married Duchess of Mecklenburg.
  • Sofia Eriksdotter, Queen of Sweden, married to King Valdemar Birgersson, born into the Estrid family.
  • Sofia Vasa, princess, daughter of King Gustav I, married Princess of Saxe-Lauenburg.
  • Hedvig Sofia of Sweden, princess, daughter of Charles XI, married Regent of Holstein-Gottorp.
  • Sofia Albertina, princess, daughter of King Adolf Frederick, abbess.
  • Sofia Magdalena, Queen of Sweden, born into the House of Oldenburg.
  • Sofia of Sweden, princess, daughter of King Gustav IV Adolf, married Princess of Baden.
  • Sofia of Nassau, Queen of Sweden and Norway, born into the House of Orange-Nassau.
  • Sofia Hellqvist, married to Prince Carl Philip and therefore titled Princess Sofia.
