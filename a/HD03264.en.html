The proposal means that a person's conduct should be taken into account to a greater extent both when residence permits are granted and when they are revoked. It should also become possible to revoke permits for other reasons in more cases than today.

Full text

The proposal suggests that the conduct (lifestyle) of a foreign national should be taken into greater consideration when deciding on the granting or revocation of a residence permit. The main purpose of the change is to create greater opportunities to remove foreign nationals from the country due to deficiencies in their conduct. Furthermore, it is proposed that residence permits may be revoked in more cases than today for other reasons. The overall objective is to achieve stricter rules for residence permits.

The proposed amendments are mainly intended to come into force on July 13, 2026.
