In this document, the government presents its assessment of the observations and the recommendation made to the government by the Swedish National Audit Office (Riksrevisionen) in the report "LSS – A municipal responsibility under state guidance and control (RiR 2026:6)." The National Audit Office has reviewed the state's legislation, support, and supervision regarding the municipalities' exercise of authority according to the Act (1993:387) on support and services for certain individuals with disabilities, abbreviated as LSS.

The National Audit Office's overall conclusion is that the state's work has not been effective. The National Audit Office makes a recommendation to the government to initiate adjustments and clarifications of certain provisions in the LSS, in order to create conditions for greater equality and better quality in the municipalities' exercise of authority, and to ensure that support is provided in accordance with its purpose. The National Audit Office also makes a recommendation to the Swedish National Board of Health and Welfare (Socialstyrelsen) to improve its support for the municipalities' exercise of authority.

The government welcomes the National Audit Office's review and agrees in part with the National Audit Office's observations and conclusions. The report makes an important contribution to the work of improving the municipalities' exercise of authority in cases under the LSS. The government believes that the National Audit Office's observations regarding the various provisions should be taken into account in connection with future changes to the LSS.

Given the measures that the government has taken and is taking, the government does not currently consider it necessary to take further action based on the recommendation made by the National Audit Office.

With this document, the government considers the National Audit Office's report to be finalized.
