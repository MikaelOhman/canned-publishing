In this document, the government outlines its assessment of the conclusions and recommendations presented in the report "Infiltration in the State – The work of three agencies to counter disloyal behavior" (RiR 2026:3) by the Swedish National Audit Office.

The Swedish National Audit Office has reviewed whether the Swedish Defence Materiel Administration, the National Agency for Public Procurement, and the Swedish Prison and Probation Service are taking effective measures to counter infiltration. The National Audit Office's overall assessment is that the work of the agencies being reviewed to counter infiltration is, as a whole, not effective.

The Swedish National Audit Office does not make any recommendations to the government. However, recommendations are made to the agencies to improve their work against infiltration.

The government welcomes the Swedish National Audit Office's review. Security work needs to be prioritized to prevent organized crime from occurring in our public institutions. The government has taken several measures that, collectively, address the areas for improvement identified in the Swedish National Audit Office's review. The government is also following up on the various development efforts being carried out in the area of infiltration through dialogue with the agencies.

With this document, the government considers the Swedish National Audit Office's report to be finalized.
