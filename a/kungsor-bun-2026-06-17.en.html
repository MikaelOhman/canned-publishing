TL;DRThe Children and Education Committee in Kungsör has decided on new regulations to enable the offering of folk high school programs for young people under the age of 18. The committee has also reviewed the finances and received information about schools and preschools.
In plain language

The Board for Children and Education has decided to introduce new guidelines for studies at folk high schools. This applies to young people under 18 who are covered by the municipality's "activity responsibility," which is a form of support for young people who need extra help with their education. Previously, there were no clear rules regarding this, but now there are specific regulations outlining when such a program can be offered.

Regarding the municipality's finances, the follow-up shows that everything appears to be on track. There are no unexpected expenses or changes in the budget at this time. However, the board has instructed the administration to continue working to ensure that the finances remain balanced.

The board has also received information about various topics, including school graduations and work related to the Malmberga preschool. It was also noted that the municipality has received a state grant to invest in language education during the school holidays in 2025. Furthermore, an agreement has been signed with the Public Employment Service.

Sammanfattning Barn- och utbildningsnämnden har gett förvaltningen i uppdrag att utreda förutsättningarna för att i undantagsfall erbjuda studier vid folkhögskola för ungdomar under 18 år inom ramen för det kommunala aktivitetsansvaret (KAA). I samband med utredningen har kontakt tagits med andra kommuner. Dessa uppger att de i enstaka fall har använt folkhögskola som en möjlig insats för ungdomar under 18 år inom KAA. Hanteringen sker dock utan formellt fastställda rutiner eller riktlinjer och har baserats på individuella bedömningar i enskilda ärenden. Mot denna bakgrund bedömer förvaltningen att det finns behov av att tydliggöra kommunens handlingsutrymme samt att ta ställning till om och hur denna typ av insats ska kunna användas. Avsaknaden av riktlinjer innebär att kommunen i dagsläget saknar en gemensam och förankrad hantering i ärenden där folkhögskola skulle kunna vara en lämplig insats.
