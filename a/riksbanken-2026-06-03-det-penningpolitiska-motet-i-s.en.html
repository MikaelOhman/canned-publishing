In plain language

The Swedish National Bank, the authority responsible for Sweden's economy, will hold a meeting in Gothenburg on September 23rd. During this meeting, they will make decisions regarding monetary policy, which concerns the amount of money circulating in the economy and the interest rates.

The decision made during the meeting will be publicly announced on September 24th at 9:30 AM. A press conference will also be held later that day at 10:30 AM, where journalists can ask questions about the decisions reached.

Since 2023, the Swedish National Bank has chosen to hold one of its eight meetings each year in different locations outside of Stockholm, varying their working environment. This year, Gothenburg is the location for the September meeting.

Full text

Press Release

The Executive Board of the Central Bank will hold its monetary policy meeting in Gothenburg on September 23rd. The meeting will take place at the premises of the County Administrative Board of Västra Götaland. The decision will be announced the following day, September 24th, at 9:30 AM. On the same day, a press conference regarding the decision will be held at the Gothenburg Chamber of Commerce, located at Parkgatan 49 in Gothenburg, at 10:30 AM.
