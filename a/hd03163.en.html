Attractive Locations – Expanding the Impact of Policy on Architecture, Form, and Design.

Source Riksdagen Read the original at Riksdagen →2026-03-17
TL;DRThe government has a new plan for how architecture, design, and form should develop in Sweden. The goal is to ensure that more people have a say in how our shared spaces look.
In plain language

The government aims to make the work related to architecture, design, and form more efficient throughout the country. They want to provide more people with the opportunity to influence how our environments and spaces are built and shaped. This involves creating beautiful places where people feel comfortable and preserving the values that already exist.

The state also wants to invest more in companies and industries that work with creativity and design. By strengthening these sectors, they hope to foster more innovation and create value for society as a whole. The government will also begin to more closely monitor whether these new goals are actually achieved.

To make this clearer, changes have been made to how government agencies operate. The responsibilities of the Swedish National Center for Architecture and Design, as well as the National Public Art Council, have now been transferred to the Moderna Museet. This is being done to create a strong and clear force in Sweden that works with art, architecture, and design.

Full text

In this document, the government presents a new direction for the work towards the national goal for architecture, form, and design, based on the principle that everyone should be given good opportunities to influence the development of the shared environment, that collaboration should be fostered, and that values should be preserved and developed.

The direction is to:

  • Promote more voices and shared responsibility, and to increasingly highlight the value of existing elements and the need for aesthetically pleasing living environments.
  • Strengthen the focus on the creative core and the societal values that strong and innovative industries in this field can create.
  • Create new forms for government initiatives, with a more systematic follow-up of goal achievement.

The document also explains the government's assessment that the integration of the functions of the Swedish Centre for Architecture and Design and the Swedish National Council for Art into Moderna Museet has created a strong and clear actor in the field of art, architecture, form, and design in Sweden.
