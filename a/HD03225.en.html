The Swedish National Audit Agency notes, among other things, that the Real Estate Agent Inspectorate's planning and selection of cases for review may pose a risk that the oversight does not sufficiently address serious problems, and that the Swedish Agency for Public Management's (Kammarkollegiet) oversight of the Real Estate Market Complaints Board is limited.
