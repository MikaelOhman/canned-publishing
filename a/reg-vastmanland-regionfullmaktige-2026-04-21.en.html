TL;DRThe regional authority is reducing its support for the "Länsbildningsförbundet" because it is now taking over the responsibility of directly distributing funds to educational associations.
In plain language

Region Västmanland has decided to reduce its financial support for the organization "Länsbildningsförbundet." This decision is not due to any wrongdoing by the organization, but rather because the region will now take over their work of distributing funds and monitoring how they are used.

It may seem confusing that the amount is being reduced despite a previous decision to allocate 100,000 Swedish krona. The remaining funds in the budget are intended for other purposes within the region's activities related to adult education.

This decision is part of a plan to make the work more efficient. By handling these tasks directly, the region can control how the money is allocated to educational associations in a different way.
