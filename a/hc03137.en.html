Prop. 2024/25:137 2 Proposed Act Amending the Act (1960:729) on Copyright in Literary and Artistic Works

Hereby, it is prescribed that sections 48, 61, and 62 of the Act (1960:729) on Copyright in Literary and Artistic Works shall have the following wording:

Current Wording Proposed Wording

48 § 1 A radio or television company has, subject to the limitations prescribed in this Act, an exclusive right to dispose of a sound radio or television broadcast by: A radio or television company has, subject to the limitations prescribed in this Act, an exclusive right to dispose of a sound radio or television broadcast by:

  1. recording the broadcast on a device that allows it to be replayed,
  2. producing copies of a recording of the broadcast,
  3. distributing copies of a recording of the broadcast to the public,
  4. allowing retransmission or a performance for the public in places open to the public for an admission fee, or
  5. allowing a recording of the broadcast to be transmitted over wired or wireless means to the public in such a way that individuals can access the recording from a location and at a time of their choosing.

The rights referred to in paragraphs 2, 3, and 5 of the first paragraph apply until the end of the fiftieth year after the year in which the broadcast took place.

The provisions of section 2, second paragraph, sections 6-9, section 11, second paragraph, 11a, 12, 13, and 15a-16, section 16a, third paragraph, sections 16e-17c, 17e, 21, 22, 25-26b, 26e, 26k-26m, 42a, 42b, 42d, and 42g-42k shall apply to sound radio and television broadcasts referred to in this section.

When a copy of a recording, according to this section, has been transferred within the European Economic Area with the company's consent, the copy may be further distributed.
