Full text

In this proposal, the government presents proposed changes to the state budget for 2026. These proposals are a consequence of the conflict in the Middle East, which is affecting fuel prices, as well as the high electricity prices and the severe cold that has resulted in high heating costs for households during the beginning of 2026.

The proposal suggests reducing the energy tax on gasoline by 82 öre per liter and reducing the energy tax on diesel by 319 kronor per cubic meter. This would bring the energy tax for gasoline and diesel in environmental class 1 down to the minimum tax level stipulated by the Energy Tax Directive. The energy tax on alkylated gasoline will be reduced as much as possible without falling below the minimum tax level. The energy tax on so-called "labeled" diesel will remain unchanged.

The proposal also suggests allocating funds for a new temporary electricity and gas price support for households for January and February 2026.
