The municipality of Bjuv has received a building permit application for the property Skromberga 12:14. The application concerns a change of use for a building, such as converting a garage into a residential house or something similar.

Since the area does not have a detailed development plan (a map that specifies what can be built), the municipality must consult with the neighbors to gather their opinions. This is called a neighborhood consultation. This is a legal requirement to ensure that those living nearby have the opportunity to express their views on changes that may affect them.

If you live in the vicinity and would like to share your opinions, please contact the building and environmental department in Bjuv. You can send an email to bygg@bjuv.se or call 042-45 85 000. Please remember to include the case number BYGG-2026-87 when you contact them.
