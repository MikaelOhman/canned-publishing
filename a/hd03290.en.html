TL;DRSweden is establishing a new authority – Sweden's Foreign Intelligence Service (UND) – which is to begin operating on 1 January 2027. In plain language

The government wants to create a new state authority for foreign intelligence: Sweden's Foreign Intelligence Service, abbreviated UND. It is to begin its work on 1 January 2027.

For the authority to operate, new rules are needed. The most important is an entirely new law on how UND may collect and process personal data – a sensitive area that is therefore regulated separately.

In addition, minor amendments are required to some twenty existing laws – for example on public employment, secrecy, signals intelligence and the protection of vital societal functions – so that they work together with the new authority.

The laws are proposed to take effect on 1 January 2027.

Full text

The bill puts forward legislation in connection with the establishment of a new authority, Sweden's Foreign Intelligence Service (UND). The authority is to begin its operations on 1 January 2027. The government proposes a new law on the processing of personal data and other necessary legislative amendments. The amendments are proposed to enter into force on 1 January 2027.

Proposed decision by the Riksdag

The government proposes that the Riksdag adopt the proposals for:

  1. a law on the processing of personal data at Sweden's Foreign Intelligence Service,
  2. a law amending the Act (1994:260) on public employment,
  3. a law amending the Act (2000:130) on defence intelligence activities,
  4. a law amending the Act (2006:939) on qualified protected identities,
  5. a law amending the Act (2008:717) on signals intelligence in defence intelligence activities,
  6. a law amending the Public Access to Information and Secrecy Act (2009:400),
  7. a law amending the Act (2009:966) on the Defence Intelligence Court,
  8. a law amending the Protection Act (2010:305),
  9. a law amending the Planning and Building Act (2010:900),
  10. a law amending the Act (2010:1011) on flammable and explosive goods,
  11. a law amending the Act (2011:846) on environmental requirements in the procurement of cars and certain road transport services,
  12. a law amending the Act (2011:1029) on procurement in the defence and security field,
  13. a law amending the Act (2013:315) on intelligent transport systems in road transport,
  14. a law amending the Act (2016:534) on measures for the expansion of broadband networks,
  15. a law amending the Act (2018:218) with supplementary provisions to the EU General Data Protection Regulation,
  16. a law amending the Radiation Protection Act (2018:396),
  17. a law amending the Act (2019:547) on the prohibition of the use of certain data to investigate crime,
  18. a law amending the Act (2019:1182) on the Security Service's processing of personal data,
  19. a law amending the Act (2021:1171) on the processing of personal data at the Armed Forces,
  20. a law amending the Act (2021:1172) on the processing of personal data at the National Defence Radio Establishment,
  21. a law amending the Act (2022:482) on electronic communications,
  22. a law amending the Weapons Act (2026:408),
  23. a law amending the Act (2026:956) on signals intelligence in defence intelligence activities in war or danger of war.
