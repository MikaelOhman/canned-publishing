In plain language

The Swedish Consumer Agency, the authority responsible for ensuring consumer rights and protection when making purchases, has experienced technical issues with its website. Due to these problems, an old news item was incorrectly sent out to people who follow their updates.

The old news item concerned a trampoline that was deemed unsafe and had already been recalled in early 2025. The Consumer Agency apologizes for the re-issuance of this information and is currently working to fix the error so that it does not happen again.

It's important to note that the warning about the trampoline is outdated, even though it recently appeared in the newsletter.
