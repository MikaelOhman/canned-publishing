June 30, 2026

The European Central Bank (ECB) today published the preliminary calendars for the Eurosystem's regular money market operations and reserve periods for 2028.

The preliminary calendar for the Eurosystem's reserve periods takes into account the Governing Council schedule for 2028, as well as the calendar for the regular money market operations.

The preliminary calendar for the Eurosystem's regular money market operations includes only main refinancing operations (MRO) and three-month longer-term refinancing operations (LTRO). It does not include any additional or ad hoc operations that may be carried out during 2028.
