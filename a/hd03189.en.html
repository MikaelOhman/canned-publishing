Certain adjustments to the income tax law have been made due to changes in compensation for newly arrived immigrants.

Source Riksdagen Read the original at Riksdagen →2026-03-17

The proposal includes suggested amendments to the Income Tax Act (1999:1229). It is proposed that the new benefit, the "child allowance," be tax-free, and that the "establishment allowance" and "housing allowance" be removed from the law.

The proposed changes are scheduled to take effect on September 1, 2026.
