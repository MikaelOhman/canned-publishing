COMMISSION IMPLEMENTING REGULATION (EU) 2026/1421

amending Implementing Regulation (EU) No 540/2011 as regards the extension of approval periods for the active substances bensulfuron-methyl, benzovindiflupyr, chlorotoluron, clodinafop-propargyl, cyprodinil, cymoxanil, dazomet, deltamethrin, diclofop-methyl, fenazaquin, fluopicolide, hymexazol, lambda-cyhalothrin, MCPA, MCPB, metaldehyde, metsulfuron-methyl, paclobutrazol, and tebuconazole.

THE EUROPEAN COMMISSION HAS ADOPTED THIS REGULATION,

having regard to the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union,

having regard to Regulation (EC) No 1107/2009 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 21 October 2009 concerning the placing of plant protection products on the market and amending or replacing certain Council Directives ( 1 ), in particular Article 17(1), and

whereas the active substances bensulfuron-methyl, benzovindiflupyr, chlorotoluron, clodinafop-propargyl, cyprodinil, cymoxanil, dazomet, deltamethrin, diclofop-methyl, fenazaquin, fluopicolide, hymexazol, lambda-cyhalothrin, MCPA, MCPB, metaldehyde, metsulfuron-methyl, paclobutrazol, and tebuconazole were approved as active substances for use in plant protection products for a limited period, and the applications for their renewal are currently being assessed pursuant to Article 14 of Regulation (EC) No 1107/2009;

whereas Commission Directive 2000/49/EC ( 2 ) included metsulfuron-methyl in Annex I to Council Directive 91/414/EEC ( 3 ) until 30 June 2011; whereas the approval of that active substance was renewed as a candidate for substitution by Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) 2016/139 ( 4 ) until 31 March 2023, and it was included in Part E of the Annex to Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) No 540/2011 ( 5 ); whereas the approval of metsulfuron-methyl has been extended twice, most recently by Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) 2024/324 ( 6 ) until 31 August 2026.
