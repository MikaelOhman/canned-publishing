In plain language

The Swedish Pensions Agency, the government agency responsible for pensions in Sweden, will be present in Visby from June 23rd to 25th. They will be there to answer questions about how pensions work and help visitors create a pension forecast.

A pension forecast is a calculation that shows you approximately how much money you will receive when you retire. The staff will be out in the area with cargo bikes, meeting people and answering questions over a cup of coffee.

You can visit them in and around Almedalen between 9:30 AM and 3:45 PM during these three days.
