In plain language

The municipal council of Norrtälje has received a new report. The report comes from the Public Guardian, which is the authority responsible for ensuring that individuals who are unable to manage their own finances or legal affairs receive assistance.

The report is based on an inspection conducted in April 2025. The purpose of this work is to ensure that those who need support receive the correct help and that their rights are protected by the municipality.

This information has now been registered in the municipality's system so that management can be aware of how the work with these individuals is progressing.
