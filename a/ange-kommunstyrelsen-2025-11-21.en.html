Source Ånge kommun Read the original at Ånge kommun →2025-11-21
TL;DRThe municipal board of Ånge has proposed selling a portion of the property located at Ovansjö 2:104 to the company Alby Ptx AB. This proposal will now be forwarded to the municipal council for a final decision.
In plain language

At its meeting on November 21, 2025, the municipal board proposed that the municipality sell a portion of land designated as Ovansjö 2:104. The buyer is the company Alby Ptx AB, and the sale will be conducted according to a pre-existing purchase agreement.

For the sale to proceed, the municipal council, which is the municipality's highest decision-making body, must approve the proposal. If they approve, the municipal manager will be tasked with carrying out the transaction. The municipal board also wants the authority to make the final decisions necessary to ensure everything proceeds smoothly.

Not all members of the municipal board agreed with the proposal. Representatives from the Sweden Democrats (SD) party recorded their opposition to the proposal, stating that the deadline for payment should not be extended.

Representatives from the Left Party (VF) also chose not to participate in the vote. They noted in the minutes that they believe the land transaction is important, but that there are still some unclear aspects regarding how the municipality is handling the process.
