Source Ånge kommun Read the original at Ånge kommun →2025-11-17
TL;DRThe municipal board of Ånge has decided not to make a decision regarding the sale of a portion of a property in Ovansjö at this time. The matter will be sent back for further review.
In plain language

The municipal council of Ånge held a digital meeting on November 17, 2025. The most important decision made at the meeting concerned a property with the address Ovansjö 2:104.

There was a proposal for the municipality to sell a portion of this property to a company called Alby Ptx AB. After discussion, the politicians decided not to approve the sale at this time.

Instead, they decided to refer the matter back. This means that the issue will be sent back to the individuals who prepared it, so they can review it again before a new decision is made.

Otherwise, the meeting focused on practical matters such as how the meeting would be conducted and the approval of the list of items to be discussed.
