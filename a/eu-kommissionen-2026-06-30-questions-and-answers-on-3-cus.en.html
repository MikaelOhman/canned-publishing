In plain language

The European Commission, the executive body of the EU, has decided on a new customs duty of €3 per item. This charge applies to packages with a value of no more than €150.

The purpose of this new rule is to simplify border control procedures and make it easier to handle cashless payments. The rule will come into effect on July 1, 2026.

The fee is charged per unit, meaning that each individual item in a package may be subject to its own charge if it is valued at less than €150.
