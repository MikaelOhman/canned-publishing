To be granted a residence permit based on family ties, the person you intend to live with must have resided in Sweden legally for at least two years, unless they are refugees or unaccompanied minors.

It will also become more difficult to obtain permission if you cannot demonstrate that you have sufficient funds to support yourself and your family. These financial requirements, known as "subsistence requirements," will apply in more situations than before, including when someone is already in Sweden and wants to extend their permit.
