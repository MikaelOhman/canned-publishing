The proposal suggests a new law regarding the provision and use of sustainable aviation fuels. This law includes supplementary provisions to the EU regulation ensuring fair competition for sustainable air transport (ReFuelEU Aviation). The law addresses issues such as penalty fees, supervision, and appeals. The draft law stipulates that the government will be authorized to issue regulations regarding penalty fees for violations of the EU regulation. The law also defines the powers of the supervisory authority in its oversight activities.
