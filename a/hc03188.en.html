A basic deduction in the risk tax.

Source Riksdagen Read the original at Riksdagen →2025-06-24

The proposal suggests introducing a basic deduction in the law regarding the risk tax for credit institutions. The proposal stipulates that a Swedish credit institution, or a foreign credit institution, must make a basic deduction from the tax base for the risk tax. The basic deduction should correspond to the threshold for tax liability applicable for the relevant tax year. If a credit institution is part of a group consisting of several credit institutions, a basic deduction must be distributed among the constituent credit institutions according to their share of the group's total tax base at the beginning of the year.

To avoid undermining the purpose of the risk tax, the proposal should be fiscally neutral. Therefore, the proposal also suggests increasing the tax rate for the risk tax from 0.06% to 0.07% of the tax base at the beginning of the year.

The new provisions are proposed to come into force on January 1, 2026.
