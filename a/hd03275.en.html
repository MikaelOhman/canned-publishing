Full text

In this proposal, the government presents proposed changes to the state budget for 2026. These proposals are a consequence of the serious geopolitical situation in Sweden's vicinity, due to Russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine. The proposals are also a result of the war in the Middle East, which, among other things, affects fuel and aviation fuel prices.

Due to Russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine, proposals are made for authorizations for the government to donate naval command and control equipment, and up to 16 JAS 39 Gripen C/D aircraft, including qualified ammunition, as well as to enter into agreements with Ukraine for the sale of Gripen E/F aircraft with associated air defense systems. The proposal also includes proposals for increased appropriations for, among other things, the acquisition and development of equipment and supplies to be donated to Ukraine, as well as for maintenance, training, and technical support in connection with this. Proposals are also made for increased authorizations to replace and acquire equipment.

The proposal also includes proposals to temporarily reduce the carbon tax on gasoline and unmarked diesel during the period of July 1 to November 30, 2026, by 2 kronor and 40 öre per liter. In addition, proposals are made for consequential changes regarding the reduction of the tax on agricultural diesel.

The proposal also includes proposals to allocate funds for support for reduced prices in public transport and measures to support the aviation and agricultural sectors. Furthermore, proposals are made to allocate funds for the temporary electricity and gas price support and to be able to introduce a high-cost protection against high electricity and gas prices.
