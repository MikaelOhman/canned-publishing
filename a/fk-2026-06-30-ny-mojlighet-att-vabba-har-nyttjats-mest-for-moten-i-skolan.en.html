In plain language

Starting in early 2026, there are three new situations where parents can receive pay while taking time off from work, a benefit known as "vab." The Swedish Social Insurance Agency (Försäkringskassan), the government agency responsible for administering these regulations, has investigated how these new options are being utilized.

Most of the nearly 2,500 people who have used the new rule have done so to attend meetings with their children at school or preschool. The majority have only been absent for a portion of the day, and it is primarily parents of school-aged children who have taken advantage of this benefit.

Although many are using the new option for school meetings, these payments represent a very small percentage of the total amount paid out for "vab" across Sweden.
