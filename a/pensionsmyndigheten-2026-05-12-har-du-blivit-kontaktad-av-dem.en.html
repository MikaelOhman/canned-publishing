May 12, 2026 | News

The Swedish Pensions Agency has commissioned the research company Demoskop to contact a random sample of individuals. The purpose of these surveys is to assess how we at the Pensions Agency can improve our processes, customer service, and overall support.

Demoskop will be contacting individuals who have applied for old-age pensions, housing supplements, or survivor's pensions in recent months. If you belong to one of these groups, you may receive a phone call, a postal letter, or a digital message via Kivra with a link to the survey. It takes approximately 10–15 minutes to complete.

Participation in the surveys is voluntary, but we would appreciate it if you choose to participate. Your responses are very valuable to us as they help us improve our operations. You will respond anonymously, and no one outside of the research team will be able to access your individual answers; only aggregated statistics will be used.

If you have any questions or comments about the survey, please contact the research company Demoskop at respondent@demoskop.se.
