Full text

In this proposal, the government presents proposed changes to the state budget for 2025. These proposals are a consequence of the deteriorating security situation in Sweden's vicinity, due to Russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine.

The proposal includes suggestions for increased authorizations to procure and develop, and subsequently donate, equipment and supplies that have been requested by Ukraine. The proposal also includes authorizations for the government to donate defense materiel that the Swedish Armed Forces can temporarily spare. Furthermore, the proposal suggests increased funding to prepare the materiel that is to be donated, as well as increased funding and authorizations to replace parts of the materiel that is proposed to be donated to Ukraine. Finally, the proposal includes suggestions for increased funding for support in the maritime sector, as well as increased funding and authorizations for support in civil defense.
