Full text

In this document, the government presents its assessment of the observations, conclusions, and recommendations made by the Swedish National Audit Agency (Riksrevisionen) in its report "From Vision to Reality – Sectoral Responsibility in Disability Policy" (RiR2024:22). The audit examined the role of the sector-responsible authorities in disability policy, and also included the government and the Agency for Participation.

The National Audit Agency's overall assessment is that the sector-responsible authorities are partially working effectively to contribute to the goals of disability policy, and that the role of the sector-responsible authority can be further developed. The National Audit Agency believes that the government needs to provide more concrete guidance on sectoral responsibility and a strategy for systematic monitoring of disability policy (S2021/06595).

The government welcomes the National Audit Agency's review and believes that the report makes an important contribution to the government's ongoing efforts to ensure that Sweden fulfills its commitments under the UN Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities. The government largely agrees with the National Audit Agency's observations and conclusions.

Following the National Audit Agency's review, the government has taken several measures, including tasking the Agency for Participation with proposing how the authorities' specific sectoral responsibility for disability policy can be incorporated into the planned action plan for 2026–2031.

With this document, the government considers the National Audit Agency's report to be finalized.
