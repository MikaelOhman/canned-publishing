Full text

The proposal suggests a new law regarding international sanctions. The proposed changes implement the EU's sanctions directive into Swedish law.

The main proposals are:

  • To increase the penalties for violations of international sanctions.
  • To criminalize minor violations of international sanctions.
  • To introduce new criminal offenses, including "sanctions violation," "sanctions offense," "aggravated sanctions offense," and "repeated sanctions offense."
  • To criminalize attempts and aiding and abetting certain sanctions offenses.
  • To introduce a reporting obligation, requiring certain authorities to report to the police or the prosecuting authority if there is reason to believe that offenses under the new law have been committed.

The new law and other legal amendments are proposed to come into effect on May 20, 2025.
