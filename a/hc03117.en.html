Full text

The Swedish National Audit Office has reviewed the admission system for students from countries outside the EU/EEA and Switzerland, as well as the organization and financing of their education, to assess its effectiveness. The National Audit Office's overall conclusion is that the system does not fully operate effectively. The National Audit Office recommends that the government review the regulation (2010:543) concerning application fees and tuition fees at universities and higher education institutions (the tuition fee regulation) to clarify the rules regarding tuition fee refunds. The National Audit Office has also made recommendations to the Swedish Council for Higher Education, the Swedish Migration Agency, and to universities and higher education institutions. The government welcomes the review and largely agrees with the National Audit Office's observations. In the report, the government outlines the measures that have been taken and that are planned, in line with the National Audit Office's observations and recommendations to the government.
