Full text

In this document, the government presents its assessment of the observations and recommendations made by the Swedish National Audit Office (Riksrevisionen) in the report "Government Implementation of the Fiscal Framework 2025" (RiR 2025:36). In the report, the National Audit Office assesses that the proposed fiscal policy is not designed in accordance with the fiscal framework and that the fiscal policy is not presented in a transparent manner. The agency believes, among other things, that budgetary discipline risks being weakened as a result of deviations from the target for public financial savings, taking into account special expenditures. The National Audit Office recommends that the government take the initiative to consider whether such deviations should continue to be permitted. The agency also recommends that the government calculate and report the impact of the proposed fiscal policy on the economic situation and increase transparency regarding the factors that affect changes in structural savings in the forecasts.

The government is continuously working to develop the content of the budget proposals in the direction recommended by the National Audit Office. However, regarding the design of the proposed fiscal policy, the government does not believe that there are grounds to reconsider the decisions that underpinned the parliamentary agreement on the financing of defense and support for Ukraine, and for the new document that describes the fiscal framework.

With this document, the government considers the review report to be finalized.
