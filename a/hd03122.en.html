In this document, the government presents its assessment of the observations and recommendations made by the Swedish National Audit Office (Riksrevisionen) in the report "Climate Policy Framework – The State's Work on Background Information and Evaluation" (RiR 2025:25).

In its review, the National Audit Office points out that the authorities' background information contains shortcomings, particularly regarding the authorities' work in preparing background information for the government's climate reports and climate action plans. The National Audit Office assesses that there is a risk that the government underestimates the need for measures to reduce emissions.

The government welcomes the National Audit Office's review of the state's work on background information and evaluation and largely agrees with the National Audit Office's description of the problem. However, the government has a slightly different assessment of the measures that should be taken. The government is continuously working on evaluating and developing the background information within the climate policy framework in order to improve the quality and clarity of the climate report and the climate action plan. For the 2026 budget year, the government has tasked the Swedish Environmental Protection Agency (Naturvårdsverket) with analyzing how the transparency, clarity, and accuracy of emission scenarios in the climate area can be improved, and with implementing improvements, for example, regarding the reporting of assumptions and uncertainties in the background information.

With this document, the government considers the National Audit Office's report to be finalized.
