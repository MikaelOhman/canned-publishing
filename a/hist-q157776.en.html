Johan II is the name of several rulers:

  • Ivan Asen II, Tsar of Bulgaria, 1218–1241
  • John II of Brabant, Duke of Brabant, 1294–1312
  • John II of France, King of France, 1350–1364
  • John II of Castile, Regent of Castile, 1406–1454
  • Ivan II of Moscow, Grand Prince of Moscow, 1353–1359
  • John II of Portugal, King of Portugal, 1481–1495
  • Hans (union king), King of Denmark, 1481–1513, of Norway, 1483–1513, and of Sweden, 1497–1501; in Sweden he is also known as John II, because his name in Latin is Johannes.
  • John II Casimir, King of Poland, 1648–1668
